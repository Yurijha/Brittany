A Peek Into Who I Am

Hello, my name is Uriane. I have been through a lot in my life. I have done everything in my power to be a good and decent man, but I am far from perfect. I have been hurt a lot over the years and it has made trusting others very difficult for me. Recently, this ugly truth has gotten in my way. I managed to hurt the love of my life emotionally in ways I wish I never had. Her name as you see here on this webpage is Brittany. She is the most beautiful person and woman that I've ever come into contact with.


Brittany is kind and generous, loving, and faithful. It takes an idiot like me to hurt someone so amazing. I have to make up for the damage that I caused because I never want to be in a world where I don't have her to cherish and love. I write all of this here because it's live on the internet and I want the one I love to see just how much I truly care about her.


As anyone reads this and most importantly Brittany, I want to make something very clear, love is precious. I hope that everyone in this world can find their Brittany. If you ever feel defeated just come back to this webpage as a reminder of what love is. It is out there and it is real. I have a special person to thank for putting up with my bullshit. Brittany you are incredible and I will shout it to the world to prove it to you.
